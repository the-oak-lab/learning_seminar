About the CMU Accessibility Lunch Seminar

This seminar series explores the evolution and future directions of accessibility research and practice. Each week, we discuss diverse facets of accessible and assistive technologies, featuring speakers from academia, industry, and beyond. The seminar is organized by the Accessibility Research Group at Carnegie Mellon University. [Sign Up Form]
